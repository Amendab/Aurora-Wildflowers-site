Care notes

Keep your piece beautiful.

Keep resin pieces indoors, away from long direct sunlight and high heat. Wipe gently with a soft cloth. Coasters are for everyday cold/warm drinks, but avoid dishwashers, boiling mugs and open flame unless a specific heat-safe finish is confirmed.

Custom note

Every flower is unique.

Natural flowers may slightly change colour while drying or curing. Tiny bubbles and organic movement can appear, making each piece one-of-a-kind.
